Ramen Noodles

Description

This recipe was passed down to me by my mother and her mother before her. This ingredients in this recipe have trascended a couple years and, until yesterday, continued to stand the test of time.

Ingredients

  • Ramen (of your choosing)
  • Water
  • Microwave

Steps

  1. Place ramen slab into body of water.
  2. Place bowl of watery ramen into microwave.
  3. Heat until edible.
