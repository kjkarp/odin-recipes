Grilled Cheese

Description

This recipe was passed down to me by my father and his father before him. This ingredients in this recipe have trascended generations and, to this day, continue to stand the test of time.

Ingredients

  • Bread (of your choosing)
  • Varying cheeses

Steps

  1. Place butter in a hot skillet and wait for it to melt.
  2. Place bread with cheese on it into skillet and wait until brown.
  3. Close sandwich.
